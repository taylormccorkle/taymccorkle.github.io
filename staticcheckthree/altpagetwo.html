Q: How many high school golfers go on to play golf in college?
A: Of the 144,000 high school golfers:

  • 2% go on to compete at the Division 1 level
  • 1.6% go on to compete at the Division 2 level
  • 2.3% go on to compete at the Division 3 level

    • In total, there are 801 colleges that offer women's golf programs, and 1,203 colleges that offer men's golf programs. Taking these totals and breaking them down by division, NCAA Division 1 holds 303 men's teams and 256 women's teams, NCAA Division 2 holds 2015 men's teams and 144 women's teams, NCAA Division 3 holds 288 men's teams and 168 women's teams, NAIA holds 181 men's teams and 140 women's teams, and Junior Colleges hold 216 men's teams and 93 women's teams.
